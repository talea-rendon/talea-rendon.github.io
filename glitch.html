Coral Submerssion

My projects focus on the theme of ocean conservation. I used this project specifically to raise awareness towards the issue of coral bleaching in our oceans. I created my models in Blender 2.8, then combining them into a scene and exporting that as a .gltf/.glb file which I was able to upload as an asset into Glitch/A-frame. I focused on modeling coral which I then put into my scene changing the colors of these objects
